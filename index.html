Who are we?

The Hotdoggers Club started as a group of friends who have played many other games before Black Desert Online. From CS:GO, to League of Legends, to other MMORPGs, we've played it all. When Black Desert Online was released on Steam, it became our new obsession.

We understand how overwhelming Black Desert Online can be to a new player, and we are here to help. We want to build a strong relationship with our guild members, and we aspire to become the ultimate guild!

Next

Why should you join?

Being in a guild should not be a chore. The Hotdoggers Club allows you to spend your time however you want. If you like to grind, cool. If you like to do life skills, that's cool too. You get to do what YOU want. There are no obligations.

There will be weekly guild activities such as guild missions, bosses, and scroll runs. All of which you are encouraged to join in but not required. You will also gain access to our guild's buff, such as +2 fishing. Best of all, you will meet all of the friendly faces!
